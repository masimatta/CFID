What is Volunteering?

Volunteering is giving your time to help someone else or a cause you care about, simply because you want to and without expecting payment. Volunteerism benefits both society at large and the individual volunteer by strengthening trust, solidarity and reciprocity among citizens, and by purposefully creating opportunities for participation
