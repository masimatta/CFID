Establishment of CFID

CFID believes that networking, collective efforts, sharing of knowledge, resources and information is a key to the success as for addressing the global developmental issues and challenges are concerned. Keeping this critical fact in mind; the organization always tries to join all such forums and networks which directly link to its mission and objectives..


Miss Ruman Malik CEO, CFID

Dr.Malik Hammad Ahmad Ex CEO,CFID

Dr. Malik Hammad Ahmad (CEO) ,CFID born and brought up in Multan-Pakistan. He has almost 17 years of professional experience focusing on Human Rights, Peace Education and Good Governance in Development Sector (ActionAid International-Pakistan, and ILEWASI-Spain) as well as in Government of Pakistan (Establishment Division); that also includes being in Academia for last 6 years teaching History, Peace and Conflict Studies in various universities of Pakistan, UK, and Spain. He has done PhD History of Peace, and Civil Resistance from the University of Warwick-UK, MA Peace, Conflict and Development from Spain, and Austria, and M.Sc History from Quaid-i-Azam University, Islamabad-Pakistan. He has published couple of articles, and a book chapter on Peace building, published by Cambridge University Press (India). He has been organizing International Summer School on Peace Education, and Child Rights since 2011 in Spain. His work expands from logistics arrangements to thematic activities of the schools.

VALUES & GUIDING PRINCIPLES

    In order to achieve its mission and to make its programs even more human-centered, CFID follows certain values and guiding principles in all its interventions and dealings. These include the following:

  • Peace & Tolerance.
  • Participation & Inclusion.
  • Transparency & Accountability.
  • Non-Discrimination.
  • Non-Violence.
  • Equity & Fairness.
  • Sharing & Openness.
