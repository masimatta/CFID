CFID believes in working service delivery as well as Advocacy & Research. Hence, followings are broad objectives of CFID:

  • Countering Violent Extremism, and Radicalisation.
  • Building, making and keeping peace in the society.
  • Promotion of Education with Special Focus on Girls.
  • Promotion of Gender Equality & Women Empowerment.
  • Provision of health and hygiene services for the vulnerable communities.
  • Promotion of Child Protection, Rights of Children & Youth Development.
  • Provision of safe and clean drinking water.
  • Provision of Sanitation Facilities and Improvement of Community Physical Infrastructure.
  • Community Based Disaster Risk Management (CBDRM), Provide timely relief assistance to disaster affected communities and assist them for their timely rehabilitation and early Recovery.
  • Promotion of Livelihoods and Explore Sustainable & Disaster Resilient Livelihood Options.
  • Promote Environment Protection and Address Climate Change Issues.
  • Promote Basic Health Facilities and Address health and immunization.
  • Support Development of Civil Society and Institutional Development.
  • Empowering farmers with knowledge and skills in agriculture sector.
  • Disseminate and share best practices for progressive agricultural farming.
